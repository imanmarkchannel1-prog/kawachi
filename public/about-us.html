About Us

Kawachi Group has been making everyday life simpler since 1992 — delivering innovative, practical, and affordable home, kitchen, wellness, and lifestyle products to customers across India and beyond.

Founded in 1992, Kawachi Group was established with a simple yet powerful mission: "To identify unresolved everyday needs and provide simplified solutions that make life easier." For over three decades, this philosophy has guided everything we design, manufacture, and deliver.

From innovative home utility products to smart kitchen solutions, wellness equipment, and space-saving furniture, Kawachi Group has consistently introduced practical products that improve everyday living — while offering exceptional value to every customer.

Our growing catalogue spans Home Utility Products, Kitchen Solutions, Wellness & Healthcare, Fitness & Yoga Accessories, Lifestyle & Comfort Products, Furniture, and Travel & Outdoor Essentials — each developed with a focus on functionality, durability, ease of use, and affordability. With new products added regularly, kawachigroup.com continues to evolve as your go-to destination for smarter living.

Our Journey & Milestones

How we evolved from a Mumbai partnership to a global B2B/B2C trade leader

1992

⚡ The Spark & Foundation

Co-founded by Sachin Vasant Dharap and partner Ajay Manohar Joglekar in Mumbai, Maharashtra as a partnership firm. They set up operations with a clear focus on introducing "problem-solving" household utilities. 🛠️

1994

🏢 First Regional Expansion

Recognizing rapid demand, they established a major branch office in Nagpur to manage direct sales and distribution across the Vidarbha and Marathwada regions. 🚀

1990s

📰 The Print & Newspaper Pioneer Era

Pioneered the direct-to-consumer mail-order model in India. They purchased extensive print ad spaces in popular English and regional Marathi newspapers, generating high-volume sales long before internet access was common. ✉️

Mid-2000s

📺 The Television Revolution

Pivoted from print media to demonstration-based television marketing. They became certified primary vendors for India's emerging major home shopping channels, including Star CJ Alive and Asianet. 🎥

2008

🤝 Strategic Partnership with Naaptol

Formed a massive, high-volume vendor alliance with the television giant Naaptol. This move allowed the group to outsource public broadcasting while focus shifted to mass manufacturing and sourcing. 🏭

2010s

💻 Digital Transformation & E-Commerce

Built their dedicated consumer store at Kawachi Group Official Website and aggressively scaled onto major digital marketplaces including Amazon India, Flipkart, and Snapdeal. 🛒

Present

🌐 B2B Digitization & Global Export

Moved heavily into bulk industrial trade by registering as top verified suppliers on IndiaMART and TradeIndia. The company expanded into international export markets across South Asia and the Middle East. 📦
